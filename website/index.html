Three sessions, one shared game

01 / design

Imagine it

Each child designs a character, an obstacle, and a piece of the world: all on paper. No screens, no code. Just markers and ideas.

02 / build & playtest

Bring it alive

Their drawings become the skin of one game everyone shares. They play it, test it, and tune the numbers until it feels right.

03 / polish & publish

Keep it forever

Final touches, then the game goes live at a real web link they can send to anyone. A souvenir of the day they made something real.

The child brings the imagination; the machine just runs it. Kids learn that a few numbers shape how a character behaves: so the computer is something they understand and command, not a mystery that belongs to grown-ups. The hand leads. The machine serves.
